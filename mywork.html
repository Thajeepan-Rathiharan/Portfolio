Insomnia interactive story

This was my final website I made at university where we were tasked to create an interactive story where the user either scrolls down a page and the story plays out, or they click on a button and the story plays out. I decided to base this off of a real life experience collecting different point of views of insomnia and made it into an interactive story using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This project was also made responsive on mobile devices and tablet devices alongside desktop devices. I found this project more challenging than the anime website where I was introduced to JavaScript, but like before I had a helpful teacher who helped me with the issues I had, but I worked on this one more independantly and came up with fixes myself but I can't take all the credit as my teacher did help me more than once.

Here is the link to the site: https://dmu-p2587267.github.io/Insomnia-Interactive-Story/, and if you're interested here is the link to my repository for this website: https://github.com/DMU-P2587267/Insomnia-Interactive-Story.
